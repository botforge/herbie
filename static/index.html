tag:
Herbie is a personal music archivist that lives in your messaging app. You send voice notes, audio files, and half-formed ideas. Herbie transcribes, names, files, versions, and connects everything — then hands you tools when you need them. It never makes music for you. It makes sure nothing you make gets lost, and it knows enough about music history and theory to point you in the right direction.
